Barbados

Barbados is a beautiful island nation in the southeastern Caribbean. It is known for its white sandy beaches, rich culture, and warm hospitality.

The island is also proud of its strong leadership, including Prime Minister Mia Mottley, a respected global advocate for climate action. Barbados has made its mark on the world not only through leadership, but also through culture and music, most notably as the birthplace on international superstar Rihanna.

There is much more to say about Barbados, but for now, this short introduction highlights just a few of the reasons why the island is so special.
